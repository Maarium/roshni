Our Mission

To provide opportunities, knowledge and an inclusive environment for empowerment of people, particularly for welfare of disadvantaged children and youth. We foster participation that will help inspire, educate and nurture a generation of young people contributing to community development.To develop a community of empowered people that play a vital role in the society. Children are the most precious asset of society as they are the picture of our future. Every parent strives hard to provide best facilities to their children for their upbringing and grooming. However, there is a large number of children who are not left with any parent or guardian. Unfortunately, the number is continuously increasing due to poverty, terrorist attacks and natural catastrophes. These children who are left with no support and guidance are most vulnerable to various hideous realities of life including hunger, begging, child labor, child militants, organ selling and other crime rings.
